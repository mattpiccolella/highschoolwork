Becoming an Officer

At the end of each school year, Junior members can decide to run for an officer position. The positions are President, Vice President, Treasurer, and Secretary. Officers are elected by the a group of teachers (advisers included) as well as the current members. The following factors, in conjunction with many others, are taken into consideration: involvement in NHS, ability to meet the point requirement, and leadership. There will be a meeting where candidates are encouraged to give a brief speech to help persuade the members and teachers to vote for them.
